metadata_fields.definicion

Proportion of people with income per consumption unit below 60% of the national median (modified OECD scale)
metadata_fields.formula \[PPRPR_{NAC}^{t} = \frac{PRPR_{NAC}^{t}}{P^{t}} \cdot 100\]

where:

$PRPR_{NAC}^{t} =$ population at risk of relative poverty considering the national poverty threshold (60% of the national median income per consumption unit (modified OECD scale) in year $t$

$P^{t} =$ total population in year $t$
metadata_fields.periodicidad

Anual
metadata_fields.observaciones

The income used in the calculation of this indicator corresponds to the year prior to the survey.

The number of consumption units in a household is calculated using the modified OECD scale, which assigns a weight of 1 to the first person aged 14 or over, a weight of 0.5 for other people aged 14 or over and a weight of 0.3 for people under 14 years of age.
