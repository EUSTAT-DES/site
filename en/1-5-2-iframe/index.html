metadata_fields.definicion

Proportion of compensation for personal injury, pecuniary loss and property damage paid and/or provisioned (pending settlement or payment) in relation to GDP at current prices
metadata_fields.formula \[INDEMPIB^{t} = \frac{(IDP^{t}+IPP^{t}+IDB^{t})/IUIPC_{refT}^{t}}{PIB^{t}} \cdot 100 \quad \mathrm t=1,\dots,\mathrm T\] \[IUIPC_{refT}^{T} = 1\] \[IUIPC_{refT}^{t-1} = IUIPC_{refT}^{t} \cdot \frac{(100+TIPC_{dic}^{t})}{100} \quad \mathrm t=\mathrm T,\mathrm T-1,\dots,\mathrm 2\]

where:

$IDP^{t} =$ Compensation for personal injuries paid and/or provisioned in year $t$ at prices of year $T$

$IPP^{t} =$ Compensation for pecuniary losses paid and/or provisioned in year $t$ at prices of year $T$

$IDB^{t} =$ Compensation for property damage paid and/or provisioned in year $t$ at prices of year $T$

$TIPC_{dic}^{t} =$ annual variation rate of the national CPI in the month of December of year $t$

$PIB^{t} =$ gross domestic product at current prices in year $t$

metadata_fields.justificacion_global

The Sendai Framework for Disaster Risk Reduction 2015-2030 was adopted by United Nations Member States in March 2015 as a global disaster risk reduction policy.

Among its goals is Goal C: Reduce direct economic losses from disasters relative to global gross domestic product (GDP) by 2030.

Achieving its goals will contribute to sustainable development and strengthen economic, social, health, and environmental resilience.

Source: United Nations Statistics Division
